About me

Graduating with a First Class in Graphic Design from Ravensbourne University London, I currently work as a Visual Storytelling Designer at the Daily Mail.

Collaborating with reporters and using tools such as Adobe Creative Cloud, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I create interactive and responsive online articles to engage global audiences. My work includes visualising breaking news and developing tools like our NHS data tracker and custom coded calculators.

At the Daily Mail, I contribute to innovative projects such as the award-winning "DEEP DIVE" series, ensuring our visuals resonate across diverse topics. Our team won the Innovation Award at the British Journalism Awards 2024.

My industry design experience ranges from construction, local government and news, showcasing my adaptability to make complex information clear and engaging for all different audiences.
